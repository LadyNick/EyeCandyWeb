In the Spring 2023 Quarter of my senior year at the University of California, Riverside, I took CS179N Senior Project in Computer Graphics. Students got into groups of 4-5 to create teams, each of which was to create and complete a game by the end of the quarter. In my team of 5, when we were discussing ideas for games, I asked repeatedly if anyone had any ideas, and I waited to present mine. When no one else presented ideas for games, I presented Eye Candy Bakery. I take full credit for the game's concept, design, characters, dialogue, gameplay, and art.
