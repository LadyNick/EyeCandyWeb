In Eye Candy Bakery, the player takes the role of a character who is in need of a job, either for the newest tech, or to prevent being kicked out. (That part was never decided) The player goes and explores outdoors in search for employment with minimum requirements and demand. Eventually the player stops at a bakery that is hiring urgently, entering and meeting Candy and Coco.

Candy is the head baker and owner of the Eye Candy Bakery. She is imaginative, energetic, and silly. She aims to be unique with anything she creates and finds everything she sees to be fascinating. As an experimentalist she values people with an eye for creativity. She is looking to hire someone to help her with a big project.

Coco is Candy's assistant baker and best and most loyal friend. Coco is more calm and collected than Candy, but isn't weak by any means. She prides herself on keeping the bakery clean and is meticulous when working with her hands. Coco previously worked as a nurse but quit due to lack of sleep, and decided to work with her best friend, making and decorating cute sweets everyday.

When the player asks about work, Candy and Coco explain the simplicity of the position and hire the player. For the player's training, the player must make a cake, with Candy and Coco as guides. The player will follow instructions using the ingredients: flour, eggs, milk, sugar, butter, flavoring, frosting, and extras for decorating.
