In the Spring 2023 Quarter of my senior year at the University of California, Riverside, I took CS179N Senior Project in Computer Graphics. Students got into groups of 4-5 to create teams, each of which was to create and complete a game by the end of the quarter. In my team of 5, when we were discussing ideas for games, I asked repeatedly if anyone had any ideas, and I waited to present mine. When no one else presented ideas for games, I presented Eye Candy Bakery. I actually had the idea for this game and gameplay before I took the class. I take full credit for the game's concept, design, characters, dialogue, gameplay, and soon art, after all the changes I'll make. The purpose of this site is to document the creation of Eye Candy Bakery from start to release.
